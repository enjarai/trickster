The Spell Ring is a powerful accessory that automatically and repeatedly casts the inscribed spell when worn. The spell is cast as the wearer, using the wearer's mana. The spell is cast every 20 ticks, or every second.

;;;;;

A Spell Ring is made much like a clock, with a spell core added.
